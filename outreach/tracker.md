## Learnings

1. **Big SEO publications = wrong target.** Generic emails (editorial@, community@) go to trash.
2. **Smaller agencies respond faster.** ALM Corp VP replied in 10h.
3. **SEO agencies want to sell, not buy.** They pitch paid placements, not tool purchases.
4. **SEO blog editors = guest post opportunities.** SearchEngineHive invited article submission.
5. **Personalized + data = best angle.** Referencing their specific article + AEO scan data = highest engagement.
6. **SaaS founders = right buyer persona.** They understand the problem, have budget, make fast decisions.
7. **50% of SaaS already has llms.txt** — the "you're missing llms.txt" hook is expiring. Pivot to deeper gaps.
