## Key Insights

### 1. Even top tech companies score poorly on AEO
Average 52% — most are "Fair" at best. The opportunity gap is massive.

### 2. Sentry.io is the clear leader
88/110 — they actively optimize for AI agents (wrote about this in our case study). Validates the "early movers win" narrative.

### 3. AI companies score surprisingly low
- **OpenAI: 23/110 (Poor)** — ironic. The company building AI agents doesn't optimize for them.
- **Anthropic: 49/110 (Fair)** — better than OpenAI but still below average for a tech leader.
- **Cloudflare: 49/110 (Fair)** — despite their markdown-for-agents initiative.

### 4. Developer tools lead the pack
Top scorers (Sentry, Linear, Datadog, Twilio) = developer-facing companies. They understand the technical requirements.

### 5. Enterprise SaaS lags
Atlassian (43), HubSpot (51), Zoom (56) = enterprise companies haven't caught on yet. **B2B opportunity.**
