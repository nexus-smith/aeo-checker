## Key Learnings

1. **Big publications = dead end.** editorial@, community@ addresses → bounced or ignored.
2. **Personal article reference = best hook.** Citing their specific content gets replies.
3. **SEO agencies are SELLERS, not BUYERS.** They want to sell us links, not buy our tool.
4. **SaaS founders = right target.** Understand SEO, care about AI, have budget.
5. **Guest post > cold pitch.** SearchEngineHive turned a cold email into a content opportunity.
6. **Response rate (20%) is strong.** Above typical 2-5% cold email rate. Personalization works.
