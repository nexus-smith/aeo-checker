## Patterns & Learnings

1. **Response rate:** 3/15 (20%) — ALM Corp (paid pitch), SearchEngineHive (guest post), Plausible (auto-reply pending human)
2. **Target evolution:** Big publications → SEO agencies → SaaS founders (each pivot gets warmer)
3. **Best performing angle:** Personalized data ("we scanned your site, you score X") + specific article reference
4. **Worst performing:** Generic value prop to generic email addresses
5. **Key insight from scan data:** ~50% of SaaS already has llms.txt → the "you're missing llms.txt" hook is expiring. Deeper gaps (FAQ schema, structured data, content structure) = new angle.
6. **Time-to-response:** ALM Corp replied in ~10h (sent 06:26, reply 12:10 EDT). Reasonable window.
