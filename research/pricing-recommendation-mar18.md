# AEO Checker — Pricing Recommendation

*Based on competitive analysis Mar 18, 2026*

---

## TL;DR

**Recommended: Freemium + SaaS tiers.** Not one-time $299.

## Why Not One-Time $299

- Market charges €85-489/mo recurring → MRR/ARR model
- One-time = no recurring revenue, no expansion, no retention metric
- $299 one-time ≈ 1.5 months of Peec Starter (€85/mo) — we'd be underpricing ourselves
- We're building toward 1M ARR → need MRR, not one-off sales
- Counter-positioning on "no subscription" worked for niche apps (BellyBuddy thesis). AEO Checker is B2B SaaS — B2B EXPECTS subscriptions.

## Recommended Tiers

| Tier | Price | Target | Features |
|------|-------|--------|----------|
| **Free** | $0 | Indie devs, bloggers | 5 checks/mo, basic report, no tracking |
| **Pro** | $49/mo | Solo marketers, small sites | 50 checks/mo, llms.txt generator, weekly tracking |
| **Business** | $199/mo | SMBs, agencies | 500 checks/mo, multi-domain, daily tracking, API |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Large orgs | Unlimited, SSO, SLA, custom agents |

### Why these numbers

- **Free tier** = acquisition funnel. AEO is new → people need to TRY it before buying. Otterly doesn't have a free tier. We grab early adopters.
- **$49/mo Pro** = undercuts Otterly Lite (€29 ≈ $32) slightly higher but with more value. Sweet spot for solo marketers.
- **$199/mo Business** = sits between Otterly Standard (€189) and Peec Pro (€205). Competitive positioning.
- **Enterprise = custom** = standard B2B playbook, David's network can sell these.

### Unique counter-positioning

Our edge isn't price — it's the **agent-first** angle:
- Otterly/Peec = "track your AI search visibility" (passive monitoring)
- AEO Checker = "optimize your site for AI agents" (active optimization)
- We're the only tool focused on **agent interoperability** (llms.txt, tool descriptions, schema.org)
- This is a DIFFERENT category from "AI search monitoring"
