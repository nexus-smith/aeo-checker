# AEO Checker — Competitive Analysis (March 2026)

*Research date: 2026-03-10. Sources: Google SERP, Bear AI blog, Atomic AGI blog, Atyla.io comparison, r/b2bmarketing Reddit thread.*

---

## Market Overview

The GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) tool market is **real, funded, and growing fast**. Multiple players are competing across enterprise and SMB segments. The term "AEO" (Answer/Agent Engine Optimization) is used by some players (Goodie AI, G2 awards) but "GEO" dominates the category naming.

**Key stat:** Of 47 GEO tools analyzed by Atyla in Jan 2026, only 2 use real server logs. The other 45 rely on API simulations (60-75% accuracy vs 95%+ for logs).

## Reddit User Sentiment (r/b2bmarketing, Feb 2026)

> "GEO / AI visibility tools still feel kinda early. A lot of them are great at showing data… but not all of them help you turn that data into 'okay, what do I do next?'"

**Key pain point: Monitoring fatigue.** Users want ACTION, not more dashboards.

Individual tool feedback:
- **Profound:** "Enterprise / research-grade. Great for stakeholders. But pricey, and you still need your own workflow."

- **GenRankEngine:** "Most actionable for 'fixing' the problem. Runs 'displacement check' showing which competitor steals your slot."

## Our Position: AEO Checker

### Current pricing
- Free check (landing page)
- $99 Basic Report
- $299 Pro Audit

### What makes us UNIQUE

1. **One-time purchase, not subscription** — Counter-positioning against subscription fatigue. Nobody else does this.
2. **Actionable fixes, not monitoring** — Like GenRankEngine but one-time. The Reddit pain is clear: "show me what to DO."
3. **AEO-specific branding** — While GEO dominates, "AEO" (Agent Engine Optimization) is our coined term. Unique positioning.
4. **Developer/founder-focused** — Not enterprise dashboards. Practical fixes.
5. **Blog content moat** — 6 blog posts building SEO authority around AEO concept.

### Competitive Gaps We Exploit

| Gap | Who has it | Our answer |
|-----|-----------|------------|
| "Data but no action" | Profound, Otterly, Peec, Scrunch | Audit = action plan with specific fixes |
| Subscription fatigue | ALL competitors | One-time purchase |
| Enterprise-only pricing | Goodie, AthenaHQ, Bear | $99-$299 accessible pricing |
| GEO naming confusion | Everyone | AEO = clearer, unique |

### Risks

1. **Category naming:** "GEO" is winning the naming battle. Our "AEO" term might confuse buyers searching for "GEO tools."
   - **Mitigation:** Use both. "AEO Checker — Generative Engine Optimization Audit"
2. **Subscription = recurring revenue:** One-time purchase limits our ARR potential.
   - **Mitigation:** Add monitoring subscription tier later (freemium → paid monitoring)
3. **No dashboard:** Competitors offer ongoing tracking. We offer a snapshot.
   - **Mitigation:** Position as "audit first, then monitor" — audit = gateway drug

1. **Pricing adjustment?** Our $299 one-time is BELOW even monthly budget tools ($39-$99/mo). Could go $499-$999 for pro audit.
