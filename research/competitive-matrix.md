## Competitor Profiles

### GeoArk (geoark.ai)
- **What:** GEO + AEO monitoring platform. Tracks brand mentions across 7 AI models (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, Grok, DeepSeek, Llama).
- **Features:** AI visibility scoring, entity tracking, answer gap analysis, citation campaigns, A/B testing for AI, schema automation, semantic review.
- **Pricing:** Free report, then subscription ($79-199/mo per MEMORY.md)
- **Positioning:** "Are AI assistants recommending your competitors?"
- **Strengths:** Multi-model monitoring, polished dashboard, strong marketing copy
- **Weakness:** Monitoring-focused (tells you the problem, doesn't fix it). Subscription model.
- **vs Us:** Complementary — they monitor, we optimize. Different price model (SaaS vs one-time).

### betterAEO (betteraeo.com)
- **What:** AEO scoring tool. 30+ ranking factors, 0-100 score, AI-powered suggestions.
- **Features:** Instant URL analysis, detailed insights, AI-powered suggestions, progress tracking.
- **Pricing:** Free analysis, paid tiers unknown. "500+ websites optimized"
- **Positioning:** "Build Authority that AI Recognizes"
- **Strengths:** No signup for basic scan, 30+ factors (more than our 7), established user base (500+)
- **Weakness:** No llms.txt check visible, no markdown-for-agents check. Broader but shallower?
- **vs Us:** DIRECT COMPETITOR. Same category (scan URL → get score). They have more factors but we have deeper agent-specific checks (llms.txt, markdown negotiation, AI bot permissions). Our data studies are a content moat.

### AEOptimer (aeoptimer.com)
- **What:** Auto-optimization service. Script-based — you place a script, they crawl and optimize.
- **Features:** Auto-crawl, trend monitoring, monthly optimization, hidden data layer for AI.
- **Pricing:** Unknown (requires account)
- **Positioning:** "We make your content easy for AI assistants to understand"
- **Strengths:** Hands-off optimization (automated), invisible to humans (only AI sees changes)
- **Weakness:** Script dependency (trust issue?), opaque optimization, no public scoring
- **vs Us:** Different approach — they auto-optimize, we audit and educate. Non-competing.

### BrandRadar (brandradar.ai)
- **What:** Full-stack AI discovery agency. Managed service.
- **Features:** Visibility audit, gap analysis, fix implementation, ongoing monitoring, analytics.
- **Pricing:** Agency pricing (likely $2K-$15K/mo based on market)
- **Positioning:** "Your brand's discovery infrastructure, managed end-to-end"
- **Strengths:** Case studies (2x lift claim), managed = no DIY, holistic approach
- **Weakness:** Agency pricing = SMB inaccessible, not self-service
- **vs Us:** Different segment — they serve enterprise, we serve DIY/SMB. Not competing directly.

### AEO Dog (aeodog.com)
- **What:** AEO tools collection. JS-heavy site, minimal info via fetch.
- **Pricing:** Unknown
- **vs Us:** Unclear feature set. May be early-stage.

### AEO Engine (aeoengine.com)
- **Status:** ❌ Domain not resolving. Possibly dead or pre-launch.

## Threats

1. **betterAEO expanding** — If they add llms.txt + markdown checks, they're a direct threat with more traction.
2. **SEO tools adding AEO** — Ahrefs, SEMrush, Moz could add AEO modules to existing platforms. Category killer risk.
3. **Free tool commoditization** — Multiple free AEO scanners erode willingness to pay for audits.
4. **GeoArk moving into optimization** — If they go from "monitor" to "monitor + fix", they cover our space.

## Strategic Implications

1. **Data moat is our edge** — Original benchmarks (top 20 scans) + public research that nobody else does. Double down.
2. **Agent-specific depth > breadth** — Don't compete on "30+ factors". Win on the 7 that matter most for AI agents.
3. **Content flywheel** — 16 blog posts + case studies + data studies. Hard to replicate. Keep building.
4. **Pricing conversation with David** — $299 one-time is unique positioning. Don't go subscription unless forced.
