## Analysis

### We're 10–40x cheaper than the market

Our $299 Pro Audit = one-time. Market charges $2,000–$3,000/MONTH for basic GEO services. Even cheapest GEO retainers start at $2K/mo.

**This is either:**
1. 🟢 **Massive value prop** — accessible to SMBs who can't afford $2K+/mo agencies
2. 🔴 **Too cheap to be taken seriously** — B2B buyers associate price with quality

### The market is split in two

1. **Agency services** ($2K–$15K/mo): Manual, consultative, relationship-driven. Our competitor only on the "audit" piece.
2. **SaaS tools** ($49–$99/mo): Automated monitoring. THIS is our real competitive set.

### Key insight: we're a TOOL, not an AGENCY

Our automated checker is comparable to SaaS monitoring tools ($49–$99/mo), NOT to $8K/mo agency retainers. Positioning as a tool is more honest and scalable.
