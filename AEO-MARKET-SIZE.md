## The Macro Shift: AI Search Is Eating Traditional Search

### Known Data Points
- **ChatGPT:** 37.5M queries/day (est. late 2025), growing ~20% QoQ
- **Perplexity:** 100M+ monthly queries (reported late 2025)
- **Google AI Overviews:** rolled out to 1B+ users, reducing click-through to websites
- **AI search share of total queries:** est. 5-8% in early 2026, projected 15-25% by 2028

### Implications
Every AI query that replaces a Google search changes HOW websites need to be optimized. Traditional SEO focuses on ranking in blue links. AEO focuses on being cited/recommended by AI agents.
